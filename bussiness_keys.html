Key Services

Provided by the iPrepare Business Faculty

Raising awareness of businesses, governments and relevant stakeholders on the critical importance of disaster resilience as part of building holistic resilience efforts

Documentation of lessons-learned and best practices on business resilience activities and innovation

Developing capacity on Business Continuity Management (BCM) through Training of Trainer initiatives & direct technical support provided to selected SMEsv

Organizing and facilitating high profile knowledge sharing platforms (e.g. regional forums, national consultations) to encourage active dialogue between key policymakers, governments, public organizations, businesses and civil society organizations

Supporting relevant governmental ministries, departments and agencies responsible for engaging with businesses, including SMEs, to integrate disaster resilience initiatives into their policies, strategies and activities
